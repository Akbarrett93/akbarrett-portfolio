About Me:

Hello there, my name is Alex Barrett. I am currently 29 years old and work in the semiconductor industry. Prior to that I worked in biotech, mostly doing material science in both areas. Right now I am an SEM analyst, which means I work with a very specific type of microscope to look at things very small. I wanted to get into coding as a way to change job fields because the possible schedule adjustment would work better for my life. I am an avid hiker, I've hiked / camped in 15 different states from North Carolina to California. My favorite place was Mount Lassen in northern California, which is actually an active volcano.
